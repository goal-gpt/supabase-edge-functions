Your Week 1 Program

Let's kickstart your journey by creating a budget:

  1. List all your sources of income and all your expenses, including student loan payments and living expenses.
  2. Consider using a budgeting app that categorizes your expenses and provides insights into your spending habits.
  3. Create a visual representation of your budget using colored markers or stickers to make it more engaging.
  4. Reward yourself with a small treat every time you stick to your budget for a week.

We believe that by following this program, you will gain a deeper understanding of your financial situation, develop healthy spending habits, and ultimately bring yourself closer to your ultimate goal.
